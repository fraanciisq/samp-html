iPhone 11

    • The iPhone 11 is a smartphone designed, manufactured, and sold by Apple Inc. It features a 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD display, A13 Bionic chip, and a dual-camera system with Ultra-Wide and Wide cameras.

    iPhone 12

    • The iPhone 12 is part of Apple's 2020 lineup, featuring a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, A14 Bionic chip, and 5G capability. It has a Ceramic Shield front cover and a dual-camera system with Night mode and Deep Fusion.

    iPhone 13

    • The iPhone 13, released in 2021, boasts a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, A15 Bionic chip, and improved dual-camera system. It introduces Cinematic mode for video recording and Photographic Styles for customizable image processing.
